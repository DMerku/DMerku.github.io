Final project for passing the course «HTML/CSS. Basics of website making»

As for final project to finish the course I’ve made simple fixed layout without any frameworks. The topic was about online-shop. Everything was made by combination of HTML and CSS.

orthodoxographia.com

Basically, the job I had to do with this website may be divided into two categories. Implementation of new features and fixing or changing existed layout.
During this work I’ve met Content Managment System — WordPress. I’ve implemented such features as possibility to show desired place in Google maps inside the post, possibility to set desired badge in the preview post card, automatically showing badge «New» if the post was made no more than 5 days ago and implementation of new section for favourite posts.
